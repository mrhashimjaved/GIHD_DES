System Capabilities
Built for fieldwork

Everything a research team needs in the field

🔒
Operator Login
Each Data Entry Operator selects their name and stream before entering data. Every record is stamped with the DEO identity and timestamp automatically.
⚡
Real-time Validation
Every field is validated on entry — range checks, required fields, and special missing codes (999 / 888 / 777 / 222) are enforced before submission.
🔁
Duplicate Detection
Records are checked against School ID, Child Name, Father Name, Roll Number and Class before saving — preventing accidental double-entry across sessions.
📊
Dual Stream Entry
Stream 1 and Stream 2 are written to separate sheets in Google Sheets automatically, keeping parallel data collection clean and separated from the start.
🗂️
SPSS-Ready Output
Columns are written in fixed variable order matching your SPSS codebook — every submission, every time — so no reordering is needed before analysis.
☁️
Google Sheets Sync
Data is submitted directly to Google Sheets via Apps Script. No server, no database — just a spreadsheet your team can access in real time from anywhere.
